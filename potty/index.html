Usage

Potty@AY displays the last known status on toilet paper, soap, and illicit activity in washrooms at AY.

Key Facts:

  • Potty@AY is crowdsourced. It relies on people reporting washrooms that have run out of inventory.
  • Potty@AY is anonymous. Any report that is filed only contains information that is displayed on the dashboard.
